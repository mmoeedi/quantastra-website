About Quantastra

Deciphering the global airspace through data and storytelling.

Welcome to Quantastra. We are a passionate group of aviation enthusiasts, data scientists, and storytellers dedicated to bringing the world of air travel to life.

Our mission is to provide clear, insightful, and engaging analysis of the global airspace. We believe that by leveraging live data from open-source networks, we can paint a picture of an industry that is always in motion.

Our Vision

We're not just about numbers; we're about the stories those numbers tell. From tracking the comeback of post-pandemic travel to exploring the technology that keeps us safe in the skies, Quantastra aims to be your first stop for aviation insights.

This blog is a project of passion. It's built for those who look up when they hear an engine, for the data-curious, and for the travelers who want to understand the vast, complex, and beautiful system that connects our world.
